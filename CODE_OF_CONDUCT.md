# coding-ethos Contributor Code of Conduct

## Purpose

`coding-ethos` is a professional open source project stewarded by Blackcat Informatics® Inc. We want collaboration here to stay technical, respectful, and productive.

## Expectations

We ask contributors to:

- stay technical and focus discussions on code, docs, testing, and design
- be respectful and critique ideas rather than people
- assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating
- keep feedback specific, actionable, and professional
- help maintain a working environment where contributors can do high-quality engineering work

## Unacceptable behavior

The following is not acceptable in project spaces such as issues, pull requests, discussions, chats, or events:

- harassment, discrimination, or threats
- personal attacks, intimidation, or bullying
- sustained disruption that derails collaboration
- publishing private information without consent
- coordinated bad-faith participation, trolling, or sealioning

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings, requests to disengage, comment moderation, or bans from project spaces. Decisions are final.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

- email <conduct@blackcat.ca>
- include links, context, and any relevant screenshots or logs
- avoid public escalation while a report is being reviewed

Reports are handled confidentially and will be acknowledged within two business days.
